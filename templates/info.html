VISION

HEAL’s vision is to create a world where place, race, and socioeconomic status do not determine health.

WHAT IS HEAL?

HEAL is an interactive web-based assessment tool that encourages users to consider public health equity when creating policies, programs, and budgets. To eliminate inequalities in public health, communities must alter their decision-making practices to prioritize equity at every opportunity.


WHAT PROBLEM DOES HEAL ADDRESS?


The American Public Health Association notes that a community’s design has direct and indirect impacts on both physical and mental health. The impacts are greater on “vulnerable populations” who are less likely to have access to nutritious, affordable food and opportunities for physical activity and are more likely to be exposed to environmental pollutants and circumstances that increase stress.

HEAL is an online platform that allows users to understand the cross-sectional nature of policies, the potential future impacts of decisions, and alternatives to existing or proposed policies.
